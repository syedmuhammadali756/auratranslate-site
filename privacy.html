The Short Version

  • We never collect your personal information
  • Your translations are not stored on any server
  • History and settings live only on your device
  • We don't use cookies, fingerprints, or trackers
  • Uninstalling the extension wipes everything

What Happens to Your Text

When you translate something, your text is sent to our AI processing partner (Longcat.chat) to generate the translation. That request is processed in real time and the result is sent back to you immediately.

Nothing is stored. The text is not logged, archived, or saved on any server. Once the translation is returned, the request is gone. There is no database of your translations anywhere outside your own browser.

Local Storage Only

Your translation history, notepad content, selected tone, and all settings are stored using Chrome's built-in local storage (chrome.storage.local). This data lives on your device and is accessible only to the AuraTranslate extension.

No other extension, website, or service can read this data. If you clear your browser data or uninstall the extension, it is permanently deleted. We have no access to it and no backup exists.

No Accounts, No Sign-ups

AuraTranslate does not require you to create an account, enter an email, or provide any identifying information. There is no login screen. There is no user ID. We don't know who you are, and we designed it that way on purpose.

Third-Party AI Processing

Translation requests are processed through Longcat.chat, a third-party AI inference provider. The text you submit for translation is sent to their API in an anonymized request. No user identifiers, browser fingerprints, or tracking data is included in these requests.

Longcat.chat processes the request and returns the result. We recommend reviewing their own privacy policy if you have specific concerns about their data handling practices.

Permissions We Use and Why

Storage: To save your translation history, notepad, and settings on your device.

Active Tab: To detect the text field you're currently typing in and inject translations into it.

Scripting: To place the floating translate button next to text fields on websites you visit.

We request the minimum permissions required for the extension to work. We do not request access to your browsing history, bookmarks, downloads, or any data beyond what's needed for translation.

Your Control

You have full control over everything. You can clear your history from the extension settings. You can disable the floating button. You can uninstall the extension at any time and every piece of data will be removed from your browser.

If you have any questions about this policy or how your data is handled, reach out to Dr Web Jr. through the extension's support channels.
